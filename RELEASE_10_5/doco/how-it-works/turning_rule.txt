To Turn the Vehicle LEFT (heading decreasing):
   When Diving:    Positive (> 0) C_BATTROLL

   When Climbing:  Negative (< 0) C_BATTROLL


To Turn the Vehicle RIGHT (heading increasing):
   When Diving:    Negative (< 0) C_BATTROLL

   When Climbing:  Positive (> 0) C_BATTROLL


----------------------------------------------------

Positive (>0) C_BATTROLL
   When Diving:    Turns vehicle Left (heading decreasing)

   When Climbing:  Turns vehicle Right(heading increasing)

Negative (<0) C_BATTROLL
   When Diving:    Turns vehicle Right(heading increasing)

   When Climbing:  Turns vehicle Left (heading decreasing)
